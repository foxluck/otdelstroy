Upgrade or downgrade your account

Click Account link at the top of your WebAsyst page to open account managment panel. Then follow Upgrade/Downgrade link.

You can upgrade your free WebAsyst account at any time and switch to a standard plan or build your own custom billing plan.

If you are already using a paid WebAsyst service, you can downgrade it to its free version. In the case of downgrade, limitations will apply (maximum number of contacts, files, photos, etc). You can remove (uninstall) certain services if not needed.

NOTE: Removing a service will permanently remove all the data associated with it.

Account name, logo, and colors

You can change your account name which is shown at the top panel, or disable it. To do so, click Account name, logo, and colors on the Account page. Then enter new text into "Account Name" field (up to 50 characters).

To place your logo at the top left corner, select GIF file on your local computer using "Browse" button. Choose color scheme for the top panel, and then click "Apply" button below.

NOTE: Adding logo is allowed only for paid accounts. For best logo view use image file no larger than 250 x 100 pixels.

Currency List

Your account includes currency list, which displays id (USD) and name (US Dollar) of every currency. Add or delete currencies to/from the list as needed.

System Settings

You can customize date format of services included in your account to be displayed as MM/DD/YYYY, MM.DD.YYYY or DD.MM.YYYY. On the same page you can also set account session expiration interval. The choices are the following: default (30 min), never expires, and custom (enter time period in minutes).

SMS History

If SMS function is enabled, the page displays SMS account balance and history of SMS activity for all account users.
