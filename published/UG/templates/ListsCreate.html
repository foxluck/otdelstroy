[`There are two kinds of lists`]:
  1. [`Static list`]

[`Consists of expressly selected contacts. Simply add individual contacts to this list. You can add or delete from this list at any time.`]

  2. [`Search-based list`]

[`Consists of contacts which satisfy certain search criteria. The list will be dynamically updated, when any contact is added, modified, or deleted from your account.`]
